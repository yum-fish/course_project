All About Tan Town:
This wonderful town sits on a
mountain in the lowest valley
in the Color Continent. It's
the nation's 519th most
populous city, known for their
vegan steaks and beef tofu.



Tan Town Frequently Asked Questions:


  1. I'm visiting for a weekend. What are some can't-miss attractions?

    2. The Beige Paint Museum is world-famous. And no trip is complete without seeing the Glacially Slow-Moving Parking Meter Installation.

  2. What's the most exciting thing to do in Tan Town?

    3. Watching the town's lone traffic light cycle through red, yellow and green. It's riveting stuff.

  3. Any notable events or festivals?

    4. You won't want to miss the spine-tingling Annual Watching Fog Roll In celebration each March. Or the edge-of-your-seat File Organization Convention in late autumn.
